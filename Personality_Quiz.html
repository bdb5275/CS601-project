About Genshin Impact And This Quiz

Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game developed by miHoYo, a Chinese game development studio. The game was released in September 2020 for multiple platforms, including Windows, PlayStation 4, and mobile platforms. The game is set in the fantasy world of Teyvat, which is divided into seven distinct nations, each inspired by different real-world cultures and elements. Characters can also gain something called Elemental Visions, granting them control over one of the seven elemental powers: Anemo (wind), Geo (earth), Electro (electricity), Dendro (nature), Hydro (water), Pyro (fire), and Cryo (ice). How characters gain visions seem to be related to their ambitions and how they strive to conquer the obstacles preventing them from achieving it.

Brandon noticed there seems to be some consistencies in how characters are given a vision and what type of vision they receive, and after extensively studying the lore and background of different characters, he was able to figure out the common denominator of how visions are given. To re-iterate, visions represents a character's ambition and drive, and most importantly how they go about navigating obstacles in order to reach their goals. This quiz seeks to replicate that process.

NOTE: It is important to distinguish your goal from your work. A cashier working at 7-Eleven likely isn't pursuing his life goals so much as merely performing a task necessary for his survival, but once he get off work to practice opera singing at home, he most likely is engaging in him desire to become a famous singer. Goals can also be long-term or short-term, such as the goal of saving one's country from terminal decline or jumping on the computer play one's favorite video game. So please keep this in mind as you complete the quiz below.

Question 1:

Imagine something you have been wanting to do for a long time and it is coming up this Sunday (ex: a cruise, a vacation, a concert of your favorite band). However, there is an incident at your house that you simply cannot ignore, perhaps a slightly leaking pipe. How would you go about dealing with this situation?

Question 2:

Which of these statements do you consider best describes your approach to life? Do not think on it, just focus on which statement resonated with you the most.

Question 3:

Picture your ideal society, which of these traits would describe it the most? Do not think on it, just pick the trait that resonated with you the most.

Question 4:

You are the director of a small team working towards a specific goal of your choosing, while all your team members are quite hard working, you noticed one of your team member appears to be underperforming relative to other members. You have noticed this since the team was formed and you have noted your concerns with that member a couple times in the past already, but have yet to see any changes. How do you proceed?

Question 5:

Imagine that you are public servant in a small kingdom. The kingdom is very clearly in decline with rising poverty rates and declining living standards. Worst of all is that the king seems completely oblivious to al this and spends most of his time hosting elaborate parties at his palace. Which course of action would you most likely take?

Question 6:

Which of these is most important to you? Don't think on it, just pick the one that resonates with you the most.

Question 7:

What is one thing about you that you are most proud of? Pick one that aligns most closely.

Question 8:

As you work towards your goals in life, you inevitably will encounter obstacles along the way. When your efforts seemingly don't yield any results, how do you typically respond to it?

Question 9:

You and your friends head over to an outdoor stadium for an event you all have been excited for (ex: favorite band, favorite speaker), however, the event was overbooked and you were denied entry to the stadium despite having purchased tickets. They offered full refunds for your tickets, but it feels like a shame to just leave. Which course action do you prefer the most?

Question 10:

Assume you were made the leader of the world, and you know that unless direct actions were taken, within a generation the polar ice caps will melt and result significant land loss. Which strategy would you immediately implement first?

Question 11:

How do you usually respond to major setbacks when working towards your goal?

Question 12:

Take the above question but instead of a temporary setback, it is a total failure. Meaning you don't get a second chance and at best you can only watch other people pursue your goal. How do you think you might respond?

Question 13:
